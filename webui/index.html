Getting Started

1
Choose your mode

Pool mode (port 3333) shares block rewards among all miners. Solo mode (port 4567) gives the entire block reward to the miner who finds it.

2
Configure your miner

Point your ASIC at stratum+tcp://<host>:<port>. Use your BCH address as the username. Any password works (or use d=DIFFICULTY to set custom difficulty).

3
Mix and match

You can point different miners to different modes. For example, your Antminer to pool mode and your Bitaxe to solo mode. No reconfiguration needed.
